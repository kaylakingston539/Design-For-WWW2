About the Exhibition

Chromatic Current is a graphic design exhibition featuring the work of Kayla Kingston. The show explores the relationship between color, typography, and digital composition through a series of posters, branding projects, and experimental visual designs.

The exhibition highlights how graphic design can communicate ideas visually through layout, form, and bold color choices. Each piece represents a different exploration of visual storytelling and contemporary design practice.

The exhibition will take place at Towson University in UU 329 on April 18, 2026 at 6:30 PM. Visitors will have the opportunity to view the work and experience the creative process behind the designs.
